Covid-19 Update

Dear Members of Spiritist Organization of Seattle.

Following government and health authorities’ recommendations, the Spiritist Organization of Seattle has moved to shut down our Sunday public meetings, hoping to slow the spread of the coronavirus. As we journey through these tumultuous times together, let us continue our commitment to Spiritual growth in a new way. Our service meetings will take place online, until futher notice. You’re warmly invited to attend our Sunday services!
